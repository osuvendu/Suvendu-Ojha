Suvendu Ojha

Research Associate-I  |  PhD in Biotechnology (Microbiology)
Bacterial Cell Division & Antimicrobial Resistance Lab · BRIC-Institute of Life Sciences, Bhubaneswar

Suvendu Ojha is a microbiologist and drug discovery researcher specialising in efflux-mediated antimicrobial resistance and transcriptional regulation of resistance and virulence in Staphylococcus aureus. His work focuses on repurposing FDA-approved drugs as efflux pump inhibitors to restore antibiotic susceptibility in multidrug-resistant pathogens, combining biochemical, molecular, and in vivo approaches. He is seeking a postdoctoral position to advance his expertise in antimicrobial resistance mechanisms and novel therapeutic strategies against drug-resistant bacterial pathogens.

Technical Expertise
Experimental Skills
💊

Drug Discovery & High-Throughput Screening

Screening of FDA-approved drugs and LOPAC compound libraries. Identified Montelukast, Sertaconazole, and Oxiconazole as potent EPIs and 10058-F4 as a biofilm inhibitor.

🧫

Antimicrobial Susceptibility & Synergy Testing

MIC/MBC (CLSI/EUCAST), E-test, checkerboard synergy, growth curves, resazurin assay, and time-kill kinetics against MDR S. aureus.

🧬

Molecular Biology & Gene Expression

Cloning, recombinant protein expression, Western blotting, qRT-PCR profiling of efflux/biofilm regulators, and EMSA for protein–DNA interactions.

⚗️

Protein Biochemistry & Biophysics

Protein purification, CD spectroscopy, isothermal titration calorimetry (ITC), and fluorimetry to characterise binding affinities in MgrA and related regulators.

🔬

Biofilm Biology & Microscopy

Crystal violet assays, CLSM/STED (WGA-FITC, SYPRO Ruby, DAPI), SEM, and fluorescence microscopy for biofilm architecture analysis.

📊

Flow Cytometry & Viability

BD Accuri C6 Plus and Beckman Coulter CytoFLEX for SYTO9/PI viability and DiOC2 membrane potential to differentiate bactericidal vs. bacteriostatic mechanisms.

🧪

Mammalian Cell Culture & Cytotoxicity

A549 and HEK293T culture, STR profiling, mycoplasma testing, and MTT assays for CC₅₀ determination and compound safety profiling.

🐭

Animal Models

Subcutaneous murine S. aureus skin infection (BALB/c), G*Power sample size analysis, CFU/g tissue quantification, and in vivo PD assessment.

📈

Statistics & Experimental Design

GraphPad Prism and Excel for ANOVA, regression, and survival curves. Power analysis for adequately powered in vivo studies with appropriate controls.

🛡️

Biosafety & Regulatory Compliance

BSL-2 and BSL-3 containment, COVID-19 diagnostics participation, and handling of highly pathogenic organisms with full regulatory compliance.
